The Founding of Newport

Newport Beach, California, was officially incorporated as a city in 1906, but its roots go back much further. The area first grew in the late 1800s when developers transformed the coastline into a small harbor town after a steamship successfully entered the bay in 1870, proving it was navigable. From those early days of fishing, shipping, and small-scale settlement, Newport quickly evolved into a thriving seaside community known for its harbor, beaches, and resort lifestyle.

Bay Adventures

Take out a boat or paddleboard into the Newport bay. The bay is the best place to go if you love the water but want to stay away from big waves and big crowds. Enjoy fishing or bird watching as you stroll along the bay.

Beach Activities

Relax under the sun at the fantastic Newport beaches. Whether you want to get your tan on or play volleyball and spikeball under the sun, Newport is full of safe and clean beaches to have fun and chill out. You can also enjoy surfing, wakeboarding, and jet skiing and boating in the ocean.
